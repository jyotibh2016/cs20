About Us

The Lavender Foundation specializes in providing education and training in various computer skills and technologies. The classes are designed for individuals of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced users, and cover a range of topics including programming, graphic design, web development, and office productivity software. With experienced instructors and hands-on training, students can learn at their own pace and gain practical experience in using various computer applications. Whether someone wants to enhance their job skills, start a new career in technology, or simply expand their personal knowledge, a computer training business can provide the resources and support they need to succeed.
